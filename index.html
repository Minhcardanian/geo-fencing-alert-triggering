1. Cluster Polygon

Use the Geoman drawing tools (top-left) to draw and edit a polygon representing the cluster area.

When finished, the polygon is automatically saved for later checks.

2. Add Circle Zones

Enter a radius (m), then click "Add Zone" and click inside the cluster to place a circular zone. (Up to 10 zones.)

3. Points A & B

Click "Set A & B", then pick 2 points inside the cluster: the first will be A (red) and the second B (blue).

A: [Not set]
B: [Not set]

4. Path & Simulation

Generate a path from A to B in N steps, then simulate the device moving along the path.
