1. Cluster Rectangle

Click "Set Cluster", then click twice on the map: 1st corner & 2nd corner.

Rectangle draws once 2 corners are chosen.

2. Add Circle Zones

Enter radius, then click "Add Zone", then click inside cluster to set center.

Up to 10 zones. Each zone is a circle with random color.

3. Points A & B

Click "Set A & B", then click 2 points inside cluster: 1st for A, 2nd for B.

A: [Not set]
B: [Not set]

4. Path & Simulation

Generate path from A→B in N steps.
